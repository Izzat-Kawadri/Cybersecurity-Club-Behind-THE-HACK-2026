## 📌 Introduction

Have you ever searched for your own name online?

You might be surprised how much information about you is **publicly available**.

This is where **OSINT (Open Source Intelligence)** comes in.

---

## 🧠 What is OSINT?

**OSINT (Open Source Intelligence)** is the process of collecting and analyzing publicly available information from the internet.

This information can come from:

* Social media platforms
* Websites and blogs
* Public records
* Search engines
* Leaked databases

---

## 🎯 Why is OSINT Important?

OSINT is used in many fields:

* 🔐 Cybersecurity (reconnaissance before attacks)
* 🕵️ Digital investigations
* 📰 Journalism
* 🏢 Business intelligence
* ⚖️ Law enforcement

In cybersecurity, OSINT is often the **first step before any attack or penetration test**.

---

## ⚠️ OSINT in Cyber Attacks

Before launching an attack, attackers gather information such as:

* Employee names
* Email addresses
* Phone numbers
* Technologies used by a company
* Internal structure

This helps them:

* Launch targeted phishing attacks
* Guess passwords
* Exploit weak points

---

## 🔎 Common OSINT Techniques

* Searching with advanced Google queries (Google Dorks)
* Analyzing social media profiles
* Checking domain and website information
* Finding leaked credentials
* Metadata analysis (images, documents)

## ⚠️ Risks of OSINT

Even though OSINT uses public data, it can be dangerous:

* Identity theft
* Targeted phishing attacks
* Social engineering
* Privacy invasion

---

## 🛡️ How to Protect Yourself

### 🔒 Reduce Your Digital Footprint

* Avoid sharing sensitive information publicly
* Limit what you post on social media

---

### ⚙️ Adjust Privacy Settings

* Make profiles private when possible
* Control who can see your information

---

### 📧 Protect Your Accounts

* Use strong passwords
* Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

---

### 🧹 Clean Old Data

* Delete unused accounts
* Remove unnecessary personal data
